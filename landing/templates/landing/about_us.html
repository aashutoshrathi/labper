About Labper

Lab Performance Evaluator

Labper will serve as a web portal that serves as a comprehensive tool to assist professors in conducting labs systematically and awarding grades unambiguously. The necessity for such a software arises from the various issues faced by students and professors alike in their respective lab courses. The lack of basic resources is common to both students and professors. The resources they lack however differ.
